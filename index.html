GOOD NEWS ABOUT JESUS

The good news about Jesus is that God sent His Son to earth to reconcile humanity with Himself, offering forgiveness for sins, new life, and eternal life through faith in Him, fulfilling ancient promises, defeating sin and death through His sacrifice and resurrection, and establishing a spiritual kingdom. It's about a personal relationship with Jesus, who is seen as the way, the truth, and the life, providing hope, light, and purpose for those who believe. Key Aspects of the Good News: God's Love & Plan:
God loved the world so much He sent Jesus (John 3:16) as a rescue plan to fix the broken relationship caused by sin. Jesus's Life & Teachings:
Jesus lived, taught, performed miracles, and showed people how to live in God's kingdom. Sacrifice & Resurrection:
Jesus's death on the cross paid the penalty for sins, and His resurrection signifies victory over death and the start of a new age. Forgiveness & New Life:
Through belief in Jesus, people receive forgiveness, are made right with God, adopted as children of God, and given the Holy Spirit. Hope & Future: It offers hope for the present and a promise of eternal life, with Jesus's return to fully establish peace and justice.
